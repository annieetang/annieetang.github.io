Hi! I'm Annie, and I'm an undergraduate student at Dartmouth College ('25) double majoring in Computer Science and Economics with a minor in Digital Arts. I'm passionate about the intersection between technology and business- how technology can aid and advance our human interactions and lead to a better, more efficient world.

I'm currently focused in Artificial Intelligence/Machine Learning, the UI/UX of webpages, digital animations, and education about these topics. I also enjoy cybersecurity and participating in CTF challenges.

When I'm not working, I love playing music, especially piano and flute, and exploring the outdoors.
